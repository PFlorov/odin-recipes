Fast Rice Pudding

Description

This is a simple and fast way to enjoy rice pudding.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • Half cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the rice, cinnamon, milk, and sugar in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir occasionally and bring mixture to a boil; cover and reduce heat to low. Cook until thick and creamy, 5 to 7 minutes.
